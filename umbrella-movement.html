Umbrella Movement

A modern, minimalistic photo essay documenting the 2014 pro-democracy protests in Hong Kong.

Protesters shield themselves with umbrellas during a tear gas incident, September 2014.
Yellow umbrellas became a symbol of passive resistance and hope.
Crowds gather in Admiralty, transforming the streets into a creative, self-organized community.

Legacy

The Umbrella Movement inspired a new generation of activists and became a global symbol of peaceful protest. Its lessons shaped future demonstrations and left a lasting mark on Hong Kong's political landscape.
